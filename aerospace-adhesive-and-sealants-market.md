<p><ul><li>Water-based</li><li>Solvent-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aerospace adhesive and sealants market is primarily divided into two types: water-based and solvent-based. Water-based adhesives are environmentally friendly, featuring lower volatile organic compounds (VOCs), making them safer for both users and the environment. They provide excellent bonding and are ideal for applications requiring flexibility. Solvent-based adhesives, on the other hand, offer strong adhesion properties and fast curing times, making them suitable for demanding aerospace applications. Both types are essential for ensuring the durability and reliability of aerospace components.</p></p>
