<p><strong>The Linear Drainage System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surface</li><li>Underground</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The linear drainage system market is categorized into surface and underground types. Surface drainage systems are designed to collect and redirect water from surfaces, such as roads and driveways, using channels or grates positioned above ground. They are commonly used in urban environments to prevent flooding and manage runoff. Conversely, underground drainage systems involve pipes and trenches buried below the ground, effectively managing excess water without compromising surface aesthetics. Both types play vital roles in efficient water management and infrastructure sustainability.</p></p>
