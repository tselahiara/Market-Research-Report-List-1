<p><p>The CMP abrasive material market is primarily categorized into silica-based and ceria-based types. Silica-based abrasives are known for their cost-effectiveness and versatility, commonly used in various semiconductor and electronic applications for their efficient material removal properties. In contrast, ceria-based abrasives offer superior polishing performance, particularly for advanced substrates, due to their ability to provide a finer finish and reduced defects. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing the quality and efficiency of chemical mechanical polishing processes in industries such as electronics.</p></p>
