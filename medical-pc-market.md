<p><p>The Medical PC (polycarbonate) market includes techniques like the Phosgene Interface Method and Melt Transesterification Method. The Phosgene Interface Method involves using phosgene gas to produce polycarbonate, allowing for precise control over the polymer’s properties. In contrast, the Melt Transesterification Method relies on the reaction of bisphenol A with diphenyl carbonate to create polycarbonate through a melting process. Both methods cater to the demand for high-performance materials used in medical devices, ensuring safety and functionality in healthcare applications.</p></p>
