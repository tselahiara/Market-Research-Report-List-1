<p><p>The automatic injectors market is primarily categorized into two types: prefilled automatic injectors and fillable automatic injectors. Prefilled automatic injectors come preloaded with medication, offering convenience and ease of use for patients who require timely drug delivery. In contrast, fillable automatic injectors allow healthcare providers or patients to fill them with the desired medication as needed, providing flexibility for varying dosages and treatment regimens. Both types enhance drug administration efficiency, improving patient adherence and reducing healthcare burdens.</p></p>
