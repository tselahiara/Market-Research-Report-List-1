<p><p>The injectable drug packaging market comprises various types, including ampules, vials, cartridges, and bottles. Ampules are sealed glass containers that provide a sterile environment for single-use drugs. Vials are versatile, often used for multiple doses and can be made from glass or plastic. Cartridges are pre-filled syringes that allow for easy administration, typically used in pen injectors. Bottles are used for larger quantities, facilitating storage and dispensing of injectable solutions, catering to various therapeutic needs.</p></p>
