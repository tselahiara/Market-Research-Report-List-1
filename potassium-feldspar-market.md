<p><p>The potassium feldspar market comprises various types, each with unique characteristics. Orthoclase is used in ceramics and glass, known for its clarity. Microcline, often found in green colors like amazonite, is valued in gemstones and decorative applications. Sanidine, commonly found in volcanic rocks, has industrial uses due to its high thermal resistance. Adularia, with its pearly luster, is appreciated in jewelry, while amazonite, recognized for its vibrant color, attracts collectors and artisans. Each type caters to specific industries and applications.</p></p>
