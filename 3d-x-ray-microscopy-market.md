<p><p>The 3D X-Ray Microscopy market encompasses three key types: Contact Micro-radiography, Projection Micro-radiography, and X-ray Diffraction. Contact Micro-radiography involves placing samples in direct contact with the detector, allowing for high-resolution imaging of thick specimens. Projection Micro-radiography captures images from multiple angles to reconstruct 3D models, providing detailed visualization of internal structures. X-ray Diffraction focuses on analyzing crystal structures and material properties, offering insights into the arrangement of atoms within a sample. Each type serves unique applications in scientific research and industry.</p></p>
