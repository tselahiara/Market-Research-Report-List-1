<p><p>The surgical lamps market comprises three main types: LED surgical lamps, CFL surgical lamps, and halogen surgical lamps. CFL surgical lamps offer moderate energy savings and good light quality but have a shorter lifespan compared to LEDs. Halogen surgical lamps produce bright white light and are known for their color rendering, though they consume more energy and generate heat.</p></p>
