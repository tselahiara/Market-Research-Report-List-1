<p><ul><li>Single-Boom</li><li>Two-Boom</li><li>Multi-Boom</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The drilling jumbo market is segmented into three main types based on the number of booms. Single-boom jumbos are designed for smaller projects, offering flexibility and mobility in narrow workings. Two-boom jumbos enhance productivity by allowing simultaneous drilling in two locations, making them ideal for medium-sized operations. Multi-boom jumbos, featuring three or more booms, are suited for large-scale mining projects, enabling extensive drilling efficiency and maximizing output in expansive areas. Each type serves distinct operational needs in the mining industry.</p></p>
