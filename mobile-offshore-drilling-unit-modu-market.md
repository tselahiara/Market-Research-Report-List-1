<p><strong>The Mobile Offshore Drilling Unit (MODU) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Jackup (Non-Floating)</li><li>Semisubmersible (Floating)</li><li>Drillship (Floating)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mobile Offshore Drilling Units (MODUs) are specialized vessels used for drilling in offshore oil and gas exploration. The market includes three primary types: Jackups, which are non-floating platforms supported by legs that elevate them above the water; Semisubmersibles, floating units anchored to the seabed, providing stability in rough seas; and Drillships, advanced floating vessels equipped with drilling equipment and designed for deepwater operations. Each type offers distinct advantages based on water depth, environmental conditions, and operational requirements.</p></p>
