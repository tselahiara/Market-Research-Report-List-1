<p><p>The flow cytometry reagents market is categorized into three main types: single-color reagents, two-color reagents, and multicolor reagents. Single-color reagents are used for basic analysis, allowing detection of one target population. Two-color reagents enable simultaneous detection of two populations, enhancing the specificity of analysis. Meanwhile, multicolor reagents facilitate the identification of multiple cell types and functions in one sample, allowing for complex analyses and detailed cellular characterization, essential in research and clinical applications.</p></p>
