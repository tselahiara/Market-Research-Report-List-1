<p><p>The solar on-grid inverter market is categorized into three main types: micro inverters, string inverters, and central inverters. Micro inverters operate at the individual panel level, optimizing energy production for each module, making them ideal for residential applications with shading issues. String inverters connect multiple panels in series, offering a cost-effective solution suitable for commercial setups. Central inverters manage large arrays efficiently, typically used in utility-scale solar installations, providing high power capacity and streamlined maintenance.</p></p>
