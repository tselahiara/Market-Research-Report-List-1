<p><p>The photocatalytic titanium dioxide market includes two primary types: powder and colloidal. Powdered titanium dioxide is widely used due to its high surface area and efficiency in applications like air and water purification, self-cleaning surfaces, and catalysts. Colloidal titanium dioxide, on the other hand, consists of fine particles suspended in a medium, offering enhanced stability and ease of use in coatings, inks, and cosmetics. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs, driven by their photocatalytic properties and environmental benefits.</p></p>
