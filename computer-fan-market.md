<p><strong>The Computer Fan Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>AC Fans</li><li>DC Fans</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The computer fan market is primarily divided into AC and DC fans, each serving distinct applications. AC fans operate on alternating current, typically used in larger systems requiring higher power and more robust cooling, such as server rooms and industrial setups. In contrast, DC fans utilize direct current, offering energy efficiency and quieter operation, making them ideal for personal computers and laptops. The choice between AC and DC fans depends on the specific cooling requirements and power availability in the system.</p></p>
