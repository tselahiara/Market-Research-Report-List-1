<p><ul><li>Hard-shell</li><li>Softshell</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bike travel case market is primarily divided into two types: hard-shell and soft-shell cases. Hard-shell cases offer maximum protection with a rigid outer shell, ideal for air travel and rugged handling, safeguarding bikes from impacts and harsh conditions. In contrast, soft-shell cases are lightweight and more flexible, making them easier to carry and store. They typically provide adequate protection for local or casual travel but may not withstand extensive rough handling. Both types cater to different traveler needs and preferences.</p></p>
