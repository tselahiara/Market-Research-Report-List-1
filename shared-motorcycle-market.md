<p><p>The shared motorcycle market can be categorized into two main types: no deposit and deposit-type markets. In a no deposit market, users can rent motorcycles on demand without upfront fees, promoting accessibility and convenience. Conversely, in a deposit-type market, customers are required to pay a refundable deposit before renting, which secures the rental and mitigates potential damages. Both models cater to different user preferences, with the no deposit option appealing to casual riders and the deposit model attracting more committed users.</p></p>
