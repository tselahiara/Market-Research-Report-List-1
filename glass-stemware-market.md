<p><p>The glass stemware market is divided into two main types: crystal glass and non-crystal glass. Crystal glass, known for its brilliance and clarity, often contains lead oxide, enhancing its refractive qualities and making it a popular choice for premium drinkware. Non-crystal glass, on the other hand, is typically made from soda-lime glass, offering durability and affordability, making it suitable for everyday use. Both types cater to different consumer preferences regarding aesthetics, functionality, and price points.</p></p>
