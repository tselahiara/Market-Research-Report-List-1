<p><p>The garbage collection truck market consists of three primary types: front loaders, rear loaders, and side loaders. Front loaders utilize a hydraulic system for efficient dumping of bins from the front, ideal for commercial waste collection. Rear loaders have a traditional design where waste is loaded from the back, suitable for residential areas. Side loaders operate with automated systems for container handling, enhancing safety and efficiency. Each type caters to specific waste management needs, impacting urban sanitation operations significantly.</p></p>
