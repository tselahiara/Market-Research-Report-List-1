<p><p>The car air conditioner filter market is segmented into single effect filters and double effect filters. Single effect filters focus on basic purification, capturing dust and larger particles, ensuring clean air circulation within the vehicle. In contrast, double effect filters offer enhanced filtration by combining multiple technologies, such as activated carbon, to eliminate odors and harmful gases while trapping finer particles. This differentiation caters to diverse consumer preferences for air quality and vehicle comfort, driving demand across the automotive sector.</p></p>
