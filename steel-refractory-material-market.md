<p><ul><li>Brick Refractories</li><li>Monolithic Refractories</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The steel refractory material market comprises two primary types: brick refractories and monolithic refractories. Brick refractories are pre-fabricated, high-temperature resistant bricks used to line furnaces and kilns, offering durability and thermal efficiency. They are ideal for structured applications requiring precise shapes. Conversely, monolithic refractories are poured or cast directly into place, providing seamless, flexible installations. They are often preferred for complex designs and repairs in steel manufacturing environments. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing production efficiency and sustainability.</p></p>
