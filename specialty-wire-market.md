<p><ul><li>Copper Clad Wire</li><li>Electroplated Wire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The specialty wire market primarily includes copper clad wire and electroplated wire, both tailored for specific applications. Copper clad wire consists of a core of a less expensive metal, such as steel, coated with copper, offering a cost-effective solution with excellent conductivity. In contrast, electroplated wire features a layer of metal applied through electrochemical processes, enhancing properties like corrosion resistance and conductivity. These wires are widely used in electronics, telecommunications, and industrial applications, meeting diverse performance requirements.</p></p>
