<p><ul><li>Triacetate Fiber</li><li>Diacetate Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The acetate yarn market primarily consists of two types: triacetate fiber and diacetate fiber. Triacetate fiber is known for its superior drape, resilience, and resistance to wrinkling, making it ideal for high-end apparel and home textiles. Diacetate fiber, while offering a similar aesthetic, has slightly lower durability and is often used in lining fabrics and blend applications. Both types are valued for their soft texture, vibrant dye affinity, and ease of care, making them popular in fashion and textiles.</p></p>
