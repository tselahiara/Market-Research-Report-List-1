<p><p>The thermocouple converter market comprises two main types: signal converters and digital converters. Signal converters are designed to transform the analog voltage signals generated by thermocouples into standardized electrical signals for further processing, typically in industrial environments. Digital converters, on the other hand, provide a more advanced option by converting thermal readings into digital format for improved accuracy and easier integration with modern control systems. Both types serve crucial roles in temperature measurement and control across various industries.</p></p>
