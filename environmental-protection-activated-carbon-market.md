Powdery activated carbon is effective for applications requiring a finer particle size, enhancing absorption rates in various processes. Honeycomb activated carbon features a unique structure that maximizes airflow and adsorption efficiency, making it ideal for industrial air treatment systems. Each type serves specific environmental protection needs.</p></p>
