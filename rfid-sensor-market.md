<p><ul><li>Low Frequency</li><li>High Frequency</li><li>Ultrahigh Frequency</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The RFID sensor market is categorized into three primary types based on frequency: Low Frequency (LF) operates at 125-134 kHz, offering short-range communication ideal for animal tracking and access control. High Frequency (HF) functions at 13.56 MHz, supporting applications like contactless payments and inventory management with mid-range capabilities. Ultra High Frequency (UHF) spans 300 MHz to 960 MHz, enabling long-range communication, making it suitable for supply chain management and logistics due to its ability to read multiple tags quickly.</p></p>
