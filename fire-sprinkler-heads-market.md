<p><ul><li>Stent</li><li>Glass Bubbles</li><li>Fusible Alloy</li><li>Heat Sink</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fire sprinkler heads market features several types: Stent, Glass Bubbles, Fusible Alloy, and Heat Sink. Stent heads employ mechanical elements to trigger activation, while Glass Bubbles utilize a heat-sensitive liquid encased in glass that bursts at certain temperatures. Fusible Alloy heads incorporate a low-melting metallic alloy to initiate discharge when heated. Heat Sink heads absorb heat before activation, allowing for controlled responses. Each type offers distinct advantages in functionality and efficiency, catering to various fire safety needs and environments.</p></p>
