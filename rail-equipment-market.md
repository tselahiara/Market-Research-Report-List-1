In contrast, light rail encompasses lower-capacity, often street-level systems that blend with other traffic and serve shorter urban routes. Both types cater to different transit needs, with heavy rail focusing on long-distance travel and high passenger volumes, while light rail emphasizes accessibility and connectivity within cities.</p></p>
