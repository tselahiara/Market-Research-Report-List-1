<p><strong>The Handheld Rangefinder Binoculars Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Roof Prism Rangefinder Binoculars</li><li>Porro Prism Rangefinder Binoculars</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Handheld rangefinder binoculars are categorized primarily into two types: Roof Prism and Porro Prism rangefinder binoculars. Roof Prism binoculars feature a compact, straight-line design, making them lightweight and easy to handle, ideal for outdoor use. In contrast, Porro Prism binoculars offer a wider field of view and better depth perception due to their offset eyepiece and objective lenses, although they are bulkier. Both types integrate rangefinding technology, catering to birdwatchers, hunters, and outdoor enthusiasts seeking precise distance measurements.</p></p>
