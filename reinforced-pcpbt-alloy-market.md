<p><p>The Reinforced PC/PBT Alloy market is categorized into two primary types based on processing methods: injection molding and extrusion molding. Injection molding involves forcing molten alloy into a mold, ideal for producing intricate shapes and high-volume components with precise tolerances. Conversely, extrusion molding involves pushing the alloy through a shaped die to create continuous profiles, such as sheets or rods. Both methods leverage the enhanced properties of reinforced PC/PBT alloys, including improved strength and thermal stability, catering to diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
