<p><p>Shaft handling equipment is essential in various industries for lifting and manipulating heavy shafts during assembly or maintenance. The market is primarily divided into two types: hydraulic and electric. Hydraulic shaft handling equipment uses fluid power for better load management, offering high lifting capacity and control, ideal for heavy-duty applications. In contrast, electric shaft handling equipment relies on electric motors, providing faster operation and reduced energy costs, making it suitable for lighter loads and environments where precision is critical.</p></p>
