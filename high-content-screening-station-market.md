<p><p>The High Content Screening Station Market is primarily divided into brightfield and fluorescence imaging types. Brightfield imaging utilizes standard light microscopy to capture cell or tissue samples without fluorescent labels, making it suitable for analyzing cell morphology. In contrast, fluorescence imaging employs fluorescent dyes or proteins to highlight specific cellular components, allowing for more detailed analysis of molecular interactions and dynamics. Both methods are essential for drug discovery, toxicology studies, and biological research, enabling researchers to assess cellular responses effectively.</p></p>
