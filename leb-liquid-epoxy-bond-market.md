<p><p>The Liquid Epoxy Bond (LEB) market encompasses two primary types: Epoxy Bond and Epoxy-Modified Bond. Epoxy Bond features high adhesion, chemical resistance, and durability, making it suitable for diverse applications including automotive and aerospace. Epoxy-Modified Bond, on the other hand, combines epoxy with other materials to enhance flexibility and impact resistance, catering to industries requiring more adaptable bonding solutions. Both types are essential for structural integrity and long-lasting performance in various manufacturing and assembly processes.</p></p>
