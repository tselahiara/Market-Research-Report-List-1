<p><p>The heat insulation film market is primarily divided into two types: endothermic films and reflective films. Endothermic films absorb heat, helping to maintain cooler indoor temperatures by reducing heat transfer from outside sources. Reflective films, on the other hand, reflect radiant heat away from buildings, enhancing energy efficiency and cooling. Both types of films contribute to energy savings and improved comfort in residential and commercial spaces, catering to diverse needs for thermal management and energy conservation.</p></p>
