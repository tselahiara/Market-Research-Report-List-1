<p><p>Dry sandpaper is an abrasive material used for smoothing surfaces by hand or with power tools. It consists of abrasive grains, such as aluminum oxide, silicon carbide, or garnet, bonded to a paper or cloth backing. Unlike wet sandpaper, dry sandpaper is designed for use without water, making it suitable for woodworking, metalworking, and automotive refinishing applications.
