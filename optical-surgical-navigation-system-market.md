<p><p>The Optical Surgical Navigation System market is categorized into two primary types: Touch Type and Touchless Type. Touch Type systems require direct interaction with touchscreen interfaces, allowing surgeons to manipulate and access navigational data during procedures. In contrast, Touchless Type systems utilize advanced optical sensors and gesture recognition technology, enabling hands-free operation. This enhances infection control and maintains sterile environments. Both types aim to improve surgical precision, efficiency, and visualization, addressing the evolving needs of modern operating rooms.</p></p>
