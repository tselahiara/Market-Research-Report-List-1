<p><p>The commercial air purifier market encompasses several types, including activated carbon, UV technology, ionizers, ozone generators, and other systems. Activated carbon purifiers absorb odors and volatile organic compounds, while UV technology uses ultraviolet light to kill bacteria and viruses. Ionizers release negative ions to attract and neutralize pollutants, whereas ozone generators produce ozone to eliminate odors and pathogens. Other market devices may combine technologies or focus on specific applications, such as HEPA filters for allergens, enhancing air quality across various environments.</p></p>
