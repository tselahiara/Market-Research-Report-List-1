<p><p>Nondestructive Testing (NDT) services encompass various techniques to evaluate materials and structures without causing damage. Ultrasonic inspection uses high-frequency sound waves to detect internal flaws and measure thickness. Radiography testing employs X-rays or gamma rays to reveal structural inconsistencies through imaging. Eddy current testing utilizes electromagnetic fields to identify conductive material discontinuities and measure surface properties. Each method serves critical roles across industries, ensuring safety, quality, and reliability in components and systems.</p></p>
