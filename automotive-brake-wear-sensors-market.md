<p><p>The automotive brake wear sensors market primarily consists of two types: electronic brake wear sensors and disc brake wear sensors. Electronic brake wear sensors utilize a digital system to monitor brake pad thickness and send alerts when replacement is necessary, enhancing safety and maintenance efficiency. Disc brake wear sensors, on the other hand, rely on mechanical mechanisms that create noise or visual signals when brake pads wear down. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring optimal braking performance and prolonging the lifespan of braking systems.</p></p>
