<p><p>The Vehicle Leveling System market is primarily divided into two types: electric leveling systems and hydraulic leveling systems. Electric leveling systems use electrically powered actuators to adjust the vehicle's height, offering precision and ease of use, often found in modern vehicles. Hydraulic leveling systems, on the other hand, utilize hydraulic fluid to achieve height adjustments, providing robust support for heavy loads and enhancing stability, typically favored in larger vehicles like trucks and RVs. Both systems contribute to improved ride comfort and safety.</p></p>
