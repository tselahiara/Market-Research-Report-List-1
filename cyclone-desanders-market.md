<p><p>Cyclone desanders are vital in oil and gas, mineral processing, and water treatment industries for effective sand removal. The market can be categorized into monoblock and multi-body parallel types. Monoblock desanders consist of a single unit offering compact design and simplicity, ideal for smaller operations. In contrast, multi-body parallel desanders comprise multiple units working simultaneously, providing higher capacity and efficiency for larger-scale applications. Both types enhance operational efficiency by ensuring optimal sand separation, thus protecting downstream equipment.</p></p>
