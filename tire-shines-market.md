<p><p>The tire shine market primarily consists of two types: water-based and silicone-based tire shines. They typically offer a subtle shine and are safe for various tire materials. In contrast, silicone-based tire shines create a high-gloss, long-lasting finish, enhancing the tire's appearance but may attract dirt and debris.
