<p><p>The rail vehicle pantograph market consists of two primary types: single-arm and double-arm pantographs. Single-arm pantographs, characterized by their lightweight and streamlined design, are often used in high-speed trains where aerodynamic efficiency is crucial. In contrast, double-arm pantographs, which provide greater stability and contact area with overhead wires, are typically employed on heavy freight and regional trains. Each type offers distinct advantages based on operational requirements, influencing their adoption across various rail systems.</p></p>
