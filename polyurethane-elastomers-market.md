<p><ul><li>Thermoplastic Elastomers</li><li>Thermosetting Elastomers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polyurethane elastomers market is primarily divided into two main types: thermoplastic elastomers (TPEs) and thermosetting elastomers. TPEs are flexible materials that remain pliable at high temperatures, allowing for easy processing and recycling, making them ideal for applications like consumer goods and automotive parts. In contrast, thermosetting elastomers are rigid and durable once cured, offering superior resistance to heat, chemicals, and wear, making them suitable for industrial applications such as seals, gaskets, and adhesives. Both types cater to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
