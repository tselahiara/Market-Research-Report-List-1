<p><p>The API oil-water separator market includes various types designed for efficient separation of oil from water. Coalescing Plate Oil Water Separators utilize inclined plates to enhance oil droplet aggregation, improving separation efficiency. Hydrocyclone Oil Water Separators leverage centrifugal force to separate oil, water, and solids effectively without moving parts. Vertical Gravity Oil Water Separators rely on gravity to separate oil from water layers, suitable for larger volumes. Each type caters to specific industrial needs and application requirements in environmental management.</p></p>
