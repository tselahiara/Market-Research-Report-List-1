<p><strong>The Low Pressure UV Curing System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spot Cure</li><li>Flood Cure</li><li>Focused Beam</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Low Pressure UV Curing System market encompasses three main types: Spot Cure, Flood Cure, and Focused Beam. Spot Cure involves targeted UV exposure on specific areas for precise curing, ideal for intricate applications. Flood Cure provides uniform UV light over a larger area, suitable for coating or bonding processes where even distribution is vital. Focused Beam technology directs a concentrated light stream, enhancing efficiency and speed while minimizing energy consumption, making it optimal for high-throughput environments.</p></p>
