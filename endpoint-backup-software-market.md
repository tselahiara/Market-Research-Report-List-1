<p><p>The endpoint backup software market is divided into two primary types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based backup offers scalability, remote access, and reduced infrastructure costs by storing data on third-party servers. This option is ideal for businesses seeking flexibility and disaster recovery. Conversely, on-premises backup involves local storage solutions on the company's own hardware, providing direct control and potentially enhanced security. This method suits organizations with strict data governance or regulatory compliance requirements, ensuring data remains within their physical infrastructure.</p></p>
