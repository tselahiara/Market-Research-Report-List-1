<p><p>The commercial and residential generators market is segmented into portable and standby generators. Portable generators are versatile, offering temporary power solutions for outdoor events, construction sites, or as backup during outages. They are easily transportable and typically fueled by gasoline or propane. In contrast, standby generators are permanently installed and automatically activate during power failures, providing uninterrupted electricity for homes and businesses. They run on natural gas or propane, ensuring reliability and convenience for long-term use.</p></p>
