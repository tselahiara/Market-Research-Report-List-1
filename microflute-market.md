<p><strong>The Microflute Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>E Type</li><li>F Type</li><li>N Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Microflute is a lightweight packaging material, and it is categorized into three main types: E Type, F Type, and N Type. E Type microflute has a fluting height of about 1.5 mm, providing good print quality and rigidity, often used for retail packaging. F Type, with a fluting height of approximately 0.6 mm, offers superior printability and is ideal for high-end packaging. N Type features a fluting height of around 2.5 mm, combining strength and flexibility, suitable for heavy-duty applications.</p></p>
