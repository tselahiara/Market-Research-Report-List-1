<p><p>The battery sealant market consists of various types, including Silane Modified Polymers (SMP), polyurethane, epoxy, and others. SMPs offer excellent adhesion and moisture resistance, making them ideal for battery applications. Polyurethane sealants provide flexibility and durability, essential for thermal expansion in batteries. Epoxy sealants are known for their strong bonding and chemical resistance, enhancing battery longevity. Other materials may include silicone and acrylate-based sealants, each catering to specific performance requirements in battery production and protection.</p></p>
