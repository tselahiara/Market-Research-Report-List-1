<p><p>The folding arm awning market is divided into two main types: manual and motorized. Manual awnings require physical effort to extend or retract, typically using a hand-crank mechanism, making them more affordable and simpler to install. In contrast, motorized awnings offer convenience and ease of use, utilizing an electric motor for quick operation at the touch of a button. Motorized options often include additional features such as remote control and weather sensors, catering to a more luxury-oriented consumer demographic.</p></p>
